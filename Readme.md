🦀 Clickbait Crab

This one not normal AI.
I calm down your titles.

🔥 What is this?

Clickbait Crab takes overhyped, dramatic, slightly unhinged internet titles…
and turns them into something a normal human brain can process again.

Paste something like:

"THIS MAN QUIT HIS JOB AND BECAME A MILLIONAIRE IN 3 MONTHS 😱🔥"

…and get:

🦀 Singlish version

Bro quit job, later make money, but not overnight lah.

📘 Clear version

He left his job and later became financially successful.

🧠 Why this exists

The internet is full of:

exaggerated claims
emotional bait
“you won’t believe this” nonsense

Clickbait Crab is a small counter-force:

Less drama. More signal.

⚙️ How it works

No magic. Just structured filtering:

Detect exaggerated / clickbait patterns
Strip emotional inflation
Reconstruct meaning in:
clear English
grounded Singlish
🦀 Crab Behavior

Sometimes the crab reacts before answering:

“Wah this one drama sia.”
“This title shouting at me.”
“Relax lah… I translate properly.”

Yes, it judges you a bit.
But it still helps.

🎮 Features
🧠 Clickbait detection (ALL CAPS, emojis, hype words)
🦀 Dual output (Singlish + Clear version)
⚡ Instant transformation (no heavy setup)
😏 Personality layer (because boring tools die)
🧪 Example

Input:

"UNEMPLOYMENT DESTROYS YOU FOREVER 😭"

Output:

Singlish

Losing job tough, but not end of the world lah.

Clear

Unemployment can be difficult, but it does not permanently destroy a person’s life.
